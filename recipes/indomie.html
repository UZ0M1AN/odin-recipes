Indomie

Description

This delicacy is easy to make and takes no more than 10 minutes to get this steamy dish on your table. Get your cooking kit ready 'cause this is gonna be a blast!

Ingredients

  • Indomie Instant Noodles
  • Red peppers
  • Onions
  • Veggies
  • Egg(s) (if you like)
  • Other condiments like carrots, etc (if you like)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Place a pot of water (according to the size of your Instant Noodles) on a stove or cooker and light it up.
  2. Wash and cut your vegetables.
  3. Wash and cut your pepper, onions, and any other condiments you wish to use.
  4. Open your Instant Noodles and put in a pot of boiling water.
  5. Add the seasoning contained in the Instant Noodles.
  6. Add the other ingredients
  7. Leave to done. You'll know this when your Indomie becomes as soft as you'd like.
  8. After a while (about 10 minutes or less), bring it down. Your Instant Noodles is ready to eat.
  9. You may boil your eggs or fry them (whichever you prefer) and place them on your Noodles and eat.
